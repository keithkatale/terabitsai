## 💡 Key Numbers to Remember

### Signal Reliability
- Single indicator: 40-50% (unreliable)
- Two indicators: 55-65% (better)
- Three+ indicators: 70-80% (good)
- + Support/resistance: 75-85% (best)

### Position Sizing
- Risk per trade: 1-2% of account maximum
- Daily loss limit: 3% of account
- Max drawdown acceptable: 25-30%
- Min risk-reward ratio: 1:2 (prefer 1:2.5)

### Expected Returns
- Momentum trading: 20-40% annual
- Mean reversion: 15-25% annual
- Arbitrage: 5-15% annual
- Volatility trading: 20-35% annual

### Win Rates
- Momentum: 45-55% win rate
- Mean reversion: 55-65% win rate
- Arbitrage: 65-80% win rate
- Volatility: 50-60% win rate

## 🔑 Critical Rules (Non-Negotiable)

1. **Always use stop losses** - Never trade without them
2. **Risk max 2% per trade** - This is the law
3. **Require 2-3 signal confirmations** - Reduce false signals
4. **Respect support/resistance** - Real traders believe in them
5. **Monitor correlation** - Don't pile up correlated positions
6. **Track everything** - What gets measured gets managed
7. **Adapt to market conditions** - One size doesn't fit all
8. **Expect 20-30% drawdowns** - This is normal
9. **Keep realistic goals** - 20-40%/year is excellent
10. **Remove emotion from decisions** - Follow the rules
